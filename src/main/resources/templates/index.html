Who We Are

Welcome to our CS 222 project: Gamify!

Our goal is to help people organize their life better by giving them the framework to gamify their life. We hope you enjoy!

What does Gamify mean?

Gamifying is the process of treating your life like a video game.

Often, it is difficult to complete daily tasks, especially if they are cumbersome or mundane. However, Gamify makes them fun to complete as you are able to quantify the progress that you make!

Track your daily tasks and stay motivated to complete your work!
